What is MatchUp about?

MatchUp was conceptualized by a group of first-year ESSEC Business School students during an entrepreneurship course. The purpose of this app is to connect ESSEC students with similar interests, values, and personalities, promoting collective activities and fostering a sense of community where students freely practice their hobbies, and collaborate with each other. By addressing the potential for exclusion arising from a significant cultural and temperamental diversity within the school, MatchUp seeks to enhance the overall student experience and create a more inclusive environment.
